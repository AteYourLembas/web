Hank Effect to screen at Women’s International Film Festival

March 13th, 2008 Posted in News, The Hank Effect (2007), Press | No Comments »

The Hank Effect at WIFF - Part of the Student Program which screens 7:00 PM Monday, March 31.

The Women’s International Film Festival takes place in Miami, FL from March 28 - April 6.

Of course, all cast and crew are welcome to go! I’ve requested information on tickets.

If you order now for the last week of March, SkyBus is available from Greensboro to Fort Lauderdale for about $120 round-trip.

Feel free to contact me with any questions. THANK YOU! WE DID IT!
