About

Tory writes, draws, and codes in Los Angeles. Her fiction has appeared in Strange Horizons, Drabblecast, and Three-Lobed Burning Eye, and her art has appeared in Strange Horizons, Apex, and Eggplant Literary Productions' Spellbound anthology. She acts as art director for Strange Horizons and writes the monthly "Chick on the Draw" column for Luna Station Quarterly. For gigs, please use the Contact form.
